#  Electricity Forecasting and Optimization Dashboard

This project is an interactive Streamlit dashboard designed to **analyze, forecast, and optimize electricity demand and supply**. It provides insights into energy production, shortage forecasting, and demand optimization, helping policymakers and analysts make data-driven energy decisions.

---

##  Features

- **Electricity Production Forecasting:**  
  Predicts future electricity generation based on historical data.

- **Electricity Shortage Forecasting:**  
  Estimates the expected supply-demand gap using demand and generation models.

- **Demand Optimization Model:**  
  Suggests optimal allocation strategies to minimize shortages and improve efficiency.

- **Energy Mix & Policy Impact Analysis:**  
  Simulates the impact of policy changes and renewable adoption on overall energy balance.
